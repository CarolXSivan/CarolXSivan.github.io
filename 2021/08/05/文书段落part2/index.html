Describe an accomplishment, event, or realization that sparked a period of personal growth and a new understanding of yourself or others. (Maximum 350 words)

I have been thinking about establishing my own pop-culture and entertainment company since Grade 10. However, I often doubt whether this idea is mature enough due to my limited knowledge of entrepreneurship and leadership ability.

Last week I took part in the Emerging Leaders Summit held by FBLA China, focusing on conducting leadership training through a range of activities.

The case study on the second day truly gave me a new understanding of myself and my teammates. The reading material was a brief introduction of how the sport brand Under Armor was established. I concluded from the text that Kevin Plank, the founder of Under Armor, used a kind of leadership strategy called ‘servant leadership’, which prioritized consumer satisfaction and loyalty. In addition, his leadership traits such as caring and innovation also sparked my thoughts. Since I want to design my own clothes brand, the process of discovering suitable fabrics is vital in order to be high-quality and differentiate from other brands. This piece of thought was inspired by the story that Kevin Plank designed the first rugby shirts with quick-drying technique, which saved the uncomfortableness of sweating. From this event, I noticed that myself actually had some thoughts of how to set up a qualified business in details.

Apart from this, during the presentation time, the confidence of the speakers taught me a valuable lesson. Appropriate body languages as well as infectious emotion were used, and every group worked out an outline of their presentation using laptops efficiently. Seeing other students’ mature performance, I realized that we all have developed some potential leadership traits for future.

After taking part in the summit, I believe that I already have some ideas for my future business, and there are plenty of merit future business leaders around me.
