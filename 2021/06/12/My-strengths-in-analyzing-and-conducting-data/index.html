My strengths in analyzing and conducting data

Knowing yourself well

required

Good leaders know themselves well. They have the ability to analyze their own strengths and weaknesses, set personal and vocational goals, and have the self-esteem, confidence, motivation, and abilities to carry them out.

​ My strengths in analyzing and conducting data

In regard of the most unique character of myself, it reminds me of my ability of analyzing and conducting data. Recently I have done an online career analysis called Morrisby, which illustrated that I owned a high percentage of numerical ability as well as an analyzing interest.

Apart from using scientific testing to prove my ability, I develop such kind of character through various ways. Last term, our Economics teacher organized a project named ‘Student Company’, requiring us to set up our own company and sell original products on the School Food Festival. I worked as Analytical Manager of my team’s company due to my efficiency in integrating and analyzing figures. My first task was to design a questionnaire to investigate what kind of creative products students would like to purchase. I asked 3 specific questions and gave out both paper and online questionnaires. Afterwards, I built pie charts and bar charts so that information could be summarized. According to my analysis, bookmarks together with badges were the products that most students looked forward to. In addition, the data lead me to the conclusion that adopting retailing was the most suitable method. My assumption turned out to be accurate in the end, which brought our company appreciable profits.

Moreover, I made a financial statement for our market sales. I read relative books such as Principles of Economics and studied the 2020 financial statement of Apple Company in order that I understood the basic format and professional terms of a financial statement. Besides, I divided the available data into groups to calculate separately, so that no false calculations were made.

To sum up, because I am a care person and are sensitive to figures, my numerical ability is relatively high. To help myself strengthen this character, I have learnt how to collect and build models to analyze data effectively.
